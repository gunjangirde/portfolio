About Me

Who am I?

I’m Gunjan Girde — a B.Tech CSE student from Nagpur with a passion for building digital things that look great and actually work. Whether it’s writing clean code or designing eye-catching visuals, I love mixing creativity with logic to solve real problems.

Over time, I’ve explored everything from Python, Java, and Tableau to tools like Canva, Photoshop, Premiere Pro, and Figma. I’ve also dived into AI, data analytics, and digital marketing — and earned 30+ certifications from places like Google, Microsoft, GitHub, Deloitte, and PMI along the way.

I’m constantly learning, experimenting, and working on side projects or freelance gigs that push me to do better. I believe you don’t need to wait for a perfect moment — you grow by showing up every day, learning something new, and building one brick at a time.

Right now, I’m focused on shaping a career that balances tech, design, and impact — something that feels meaningful, not just impres.sive on paper.
